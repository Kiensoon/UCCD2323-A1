Recommended Book to Read

The New Climate War

Michael E. Manna

ABOUT

A fascinating untangling of the intricate web of misinformation, misdirection and deflection perpetuated by the fossil fuel industry since climate change became an incontrovertible reality.

Post Growth

Tim Jackson

ABOUT

The objective of this book is to debunk the “myth of growth”. Most eloquently summed up by the Chinese philosopher Lao Tzu, Jackson stresses the imperative of understanding “that enough's enough is enough to know”.

Climate Restoration

Peter Fiekowsky

ABOUT

Peter Fiekowsky's mission in life is “to leave a world we're proud of to our children”. This is the ultimate reason why, as an MIT-educated physicist and engineer, a philanthropist, a social innovator and an entrepreneur, he has been researching how to restore our climate for at least a decade.
